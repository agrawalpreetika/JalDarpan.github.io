Did You Know ?

Explore some fascinating insights about water management and the innovative approaches that have been adopted to tackle waterlogging challenges throughout history and in modern times.

  • Historical Solutions: Ancient Indian civilizations, like those in the Indus Valley, had advanced water management systems. They constructed sophisticated drainage and reservoir systems to manage water effectively.
  • Technology Integration: The use of technology, such as satellite imagery and IoT sensors, is helping to monitor and manage waterlogging more effectively by providing real-time data and predictive insights.
  • Innovative Solutions: n some areas, innovative solutions like rainwater harvesting and pervious pavements are being used to address waterlogging issues and improve drainage.
